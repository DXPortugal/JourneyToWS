The Program

Journey to the Web Summit is a Microsoft Program designed to support startups in making the most of Websummit as well as showcase the top European Startups. The Program comprises two initiatives:

Startup Challenge

Where startups meeting specific criteria from all over Europe can apply. A jury will then select the the top 2 startups who will get the chance to showcase their solutions at the Microsoft Booth During the Websummit.

Know more about eligibility and apply>>

Open Online Masterclasses

Whether or not you are applying for the Startup Challenge, Websummit presents a great opportunity to network with relevant people from all over the globe as well as to showcase your startup to the world! To help you make the most of it, we’ve created an agenda packed with online masterclasses on business and technical topics such as Growth Hacking, Pitching, Networking, IoT, Machine Learning and much more.
