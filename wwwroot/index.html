The Program

Web Summit is around the corner. Are you ready?

We've created the Journey to the Web Summit Program to help you get ready and showcase your business to the world (well, maybe not world, but how about more than 50,000 of the sharpest minds, biggest investors and the ones doing the next-gen thinking out there?)!

There are 2 ways to get involved, so choose your own adventure (and if you are based in Portugal choose both!).

#MicrosoftJWS

Startup Challenge

Online Masterclasses

No matter where you live, you can attend a set of online masterclasses that you can join for free on a range of hot business and tech topics to help you get ready for Web Summit.
