What is the Journey to the Web Summit Program?

The Journey to Web Summit Program is a Microsoft Program designed to support and select the best European Startups and showcase them at the Microsoft booth during the Web Summit conference that will take place in Lisbon later this year. The program will consist of

Who can participate?

Startups from eligible countries who meet the following criteria:

  • are registered in the Microsoft BizSpark Program
  • have a product or are working on a Microsoft technology version of a product that can be showcased at the Microsoft booth during Web Summit

Note that using Microsoft technology is not a requirement to participate but all the proposed solutions will be evaluated according to the potential and/or the interest in adopting Microsoft technologies.

Which countries are eligible?

The eligible countries are Portugal, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Sweden and Finland.

Startups from other countries are not excluded but need to be approved by a local BizSpark Manager before joining the Program. To reach out to your local BizSpark Manager visit this link.

Who will decide which startups will be showcased?

Microsoft and a set of partners will evaluate the participant Startups. The jury evaluating applications will be comprised of international venture capitalists, startup accelerators and Microsoft technical advisors.

How can I participate?

To apply for the startup challenge, send us an email to thejourney@microsoft.com. You should include a brief pitch deck or video that describes your product or solution and how you are going to leverage Microsoft technology. You can apply from September 1st through September 18th. If you are not registered for the Microsoft BizSpark Program you should register in advance in this link.

Ok, I’ve done all the steps and am registered for the Journey to the Websummit Program. What’s next?

After receiving all applications from all countries, each country will have a jury to select 5 startups. If you are on the 5 startup shortlist you will receive a welcome e-mail with more details about the Program and next steps.

Throughout September and October, our teams and partners will also be delivering a set of open online masterclasses that you can attend to help you prepare and make the most out of websummit, with topics ranging from Growth Hacking, Investment 101 to more technical sessions on IoT, Bots and Machine Learning. You can see the full list of upcoming sessions and watch them live here.
