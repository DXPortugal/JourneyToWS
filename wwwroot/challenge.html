Startup Challenge

Startup Challenge

Enter the challenge and get the chance to showcase your solution in the Microsoft Booth at Websummit as well as exclusive access to promotion and networking opportunities during the event.

The winning startups will also get free trips to Lisbon and accommodation for the event, Websummit entrance tickets and exclusive access to networking events with VCs, CIOs and top customers from Europe and the US.

Eligibility

To apply for the Startup Challenge your company needs to be based in one of the eligible countries and meet the following criteria:

  • being a member of the Microsoft BizSpark Program
  • have a product or solution (or working on a Microsoft technology version of a product or solution) that can be showcased at the Microsoft booth during Web Summit

Note that using Microsoft technology is not a requirement to participate but all the proposed solutions will be evaluated according to the potential and/or the interest in adopting Microsoft technologies

Eligible Countries: Portugal, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Sweden and Finland

Startups from other countries are not excluded but need to be approved by a local BizSpark Manager before joining the Program. To reach out to your local BizSpark Manager visit this link.

How to apply

To apply for the startup challenge, send us an email.

Your application should include a brief pitch deck or video that describes your product or solution and how you are going to leverage Microsoft technology.

We will be receiving applications from September 12th through September 28th.

If you are not registered for the Microsoft BizSpark Program you should register in advance in this link.

We will be receiving applications from eligible countries from the 1st to the 18th of September (see above how you can apply).

Shortlisting | 30th September

After receiving all applications a local jury will select 5 startups from each country. If you are in the 5 startup shortlist you will receive a welcome e-mail with more details and next steps.

Technical CheckPoint & Finalist Selection | 18th October

The 5 solutions from each country will be evaluated on an onsite / Skype pitch and evaluated by an international jury comprised of international venture capitalists, startup accelerators and Microsoft Technical Advisors. This juri will choose 1 finalist to represent each country in the Microsoft Web Summit Booth.

Program Awards

An international jury of VCs, Accelerators and Microsoft Specialists will be selecting 1 winner from each participating country. Winners will receive the following awards and benefits:

  • Websummit Entrance for up to 3 startup members
  • Showcase space at Microsoft Websummit booth
  • Access to Microsoft Websummit Pop-Up Reactor where you will have a dedicated space near the Websummit venue to meet privately with VCs, accelerators, enterprise customers and other relevant websummit attendees
  • Entrance to invite-only Microsoft @ Websummit reception, in partnership with the US Embassy, where you will get the chance to meet with top CIOs, Investors and Accelerators from Europe and the US
  • Inclusion of your startup in the Microsoft European Startup Showcase booklet to be distributed before, during and after the Websummit at the Microsoft Booth, Microsoft Pop Up Reactor, Reception Party and digitally promoted by all participating Microsoft Subsidiaries
