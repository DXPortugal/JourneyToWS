Startup Challenge

Startup Challenge

Enter the challenge and get the chance to showcase your solution in the Microsoft Booth at Websummit as well as exclusive access to promotion and networking opportunities during the event.

The winning startups will also get free trips to Lisbon and accommodation for the event, Websummit entrance tickets and exclusive access to networking events with VCs, CIOs and top customers from Europe and the US.

Eligibility

To apply for the Startup Challenge your company needs to be based in Portugal and meet the following criteria:

  • being a member of the Microsoft BizSpark Program
  • have a product or solution (or working on a Microsoft technology version of a product or solution) that can be showcased at the Microsoft booth during Web Summit

Note that using Microsoft technology is not a requirement to participate but all the proposed solutions will be evaluated according to the potential and/or the interest in adopting Microsoft technologies

Startups from other countries should reach out to us and we will connect you with your local Microsoft team.

Program Awards

An international jury of VCs, Accelerators and Microsoft Specialists will be selecting 1 winning startup, who will receive the following benefits:

  • Websummit Entrance for up to 3 startup members
  • Showcase space at Microsoft Websummit booth
  • Access to Microsoft Websummit Pop-Up Reactor where you will have a dedicated space near the Websummit venue to meet privately with VCs, accelerators, enterprise customers and other relevant websummit attendees
  • Entrance to invite-only Microsoft @ Websummit reception, in partnership with the US Embassy, where you will get the chance to meet with top CIOs, Investors and Accelerators from Europe and the US
  • Inclusion of your startup in the Microsoft European Startup Showcase booklet to be distributed before, during and after the Websummit at the Microsoft Booth, Microsoft Pop Up Reactor, Reception Party and digitally promoted by all participating Microsoft Subsidiaries
