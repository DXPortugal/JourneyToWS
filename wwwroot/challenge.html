Startup Challenge

Powered by:

We've partnered with the US Embassy to find the best of the best startups in Portugal and give you access to exclusive benefits to leverage Web Summit and expand your business!

Sign up, get chosen, win! Get a showcase space at the Microsoft Booth, passes to Web Summit for up to 3 employees, exclusive access to networking events with VCs, CIOs and top customers from Europe and the US, among many other benefits by our partners (like TAP, Canopy City, Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati and more!).


Eligibility

To apply for the Startup Challenge your company needs to be based in Portugal and meet the following criteria:

  • being a member of the Microsoft BizSpark Program
  • having a product or solution (or working on a Microsoft technology version of a product or solution) that can be showcased at the Microsoft booth during Web Summit

Note that using Microsoft technology is not a requirement to participate but all the proposed solutions will be evaluated according to the potential and/or the interest in adopting Microsoft technologies.

Startups from other countries should reach out to us and we will connect you with your local Microsoft team.

Program Milestones

Applications Deadline | 2nd October

We will be receiving applications through October 2nd (see below how you can apply).

Shortlisting | 5th October

After receiving all applications, a local jury will select 8 finalists. You will receive an e-mail communication after this date and if you are among the 8 finalists we will provide you with more details and next steps.

Final Pitch & Winner Selection | 18th October

The 8 solutions on the shortlist will be invited to an in-person event at the Microsoft offices in Lisbon where a jury comprised of international venture capitalists, startup accelerators and Microsoft Technical Advisors will select the winner.
